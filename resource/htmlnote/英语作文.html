                                                                                                                                            • 作文功能句

                                                                                                                                            自我介绍

                                                                                                                                            As a...who..., I...

                                                                                                                                            • As a senior student at this university who is particularly fond of reading in this library, I ...

                                                                                                                                            I am ...who ...

                                                                                                                                            • I am LiMing, an international student from China who will study in your country.

                                                                                                                                            寒暄

                                                                                                                                            It brings joy to my heart when I...

                                                                                                                                            • It brings joy to my heart when I recall the past two months in which I worked as an editor for your magazine.

                                                                                                                                            I am particularly/quite pleased to...

                                                                                                                                            • I am quite pleased to recall the past two months when I worked as an editor for your magazine.

                                                                                                                                            It's so great to...

                                                                                                                                            （通知/写信）的目的

                                                                                                                                            I am writing for the purpose of...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I am writing for the purpose of making suggestions for improving the service here.

                                                                                                                                            sth. is/are for the purpose of...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：This notice is for the purpose of recruiting 25 volunteers.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：...people tend to purchase presents to visit relatives and friends for the purpose of expressing their sincere wishes.

                                                                                                                                            事件背景

                                                                                                                                            ...is scheduled to begin/start...(time)...(place), which will last for ...(length of time)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：This ceremony is scheduled to begin at 9:00 am on June 26th in the Center for Student Activities, which will last for approximately two hours.

                                                                                                                                            ...is scheduled to be held...(place)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：The international conference on globalization is scheduled to be held in our university.

                                                                                                                                            ...is arranged ...(date)...(time)...(place)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：The ceremony is arranged on June 26th at 9:00 am in the Center for Student Activities.

                                                                                                                                            解释原因

                                                                                                                                            ...is exactly/precisely the reason/quality that leads/enables/causes sb. to...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Their pessimistic attitude is exactly the reason that enables them to see no hope, no chance or no possibility.

                                                                                                                                            It is...that makes/leads/enables/causes sb. to...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：It is your devoted work taht enables us to enjoy a positive learning atmosphere here.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：It is their pessimistic attitude that causes them to see no hope, no chance, or no possibility of change.

                                                                                                                                            说明自己忙碌

                                                                                                                                            ...I was extremely/exceedingly busy during...(time) because/since I focused all my attention on...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：As you know, during the past six months, I was exceedingly busy since I focused all my attention on the preparation for the national entrance exams for the postgraduate studies.

                                                                                                                                            ...I was fully occupied with...during ...(time)

                                                                                                                                            表达感谢或不满

                                                                                                                                            I am quite grateful/obliged to sb. for

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I want to say I am quite obliged to my teacher for his guidance.

                                                                                                                                            I am rather/much disappointed/indignant with...

                                                                                                                                            • 常用于投诉

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I am rather disappointed with the quality of your products.

                                                                                                                                            I really appreciate...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I really appreciate of your hard work.

                                                                                                                                            I am writing to express my dismay with...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I am writing to express my dismay with your products' inferior quality.

                                                                                                                                            值得去做/关注及其原因

                                                                                                                                            ... is particularly worthy of (doing)...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：This movie is particularly worthy of watching for two reasons.

                                                                                                                                            ...is particularly concerning for the reason that...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：These two pictures are particularly worth concerning for the reason that they foucs on how parents should educate their kids in life and study.

                                                                                                                                            展望未来

                                                                                                                                            If sb. endeavor/do their utmost, the future of ... will be hopeful/promising.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：If they endeavor their utmost, the future of the mobile phone industry will be promising.

                                                                                                                                            提建议

                                                                                                                                            I suggest that...

                                                                                                                                            ...is/are (not) supposed to...

                                                                                                                                            It is (not) advisible/appropriate for sb. to do sth.

                                                                                                                                            (As a result,) it is essential/necessary (for sb.) to (focus attention on)..., due to...

                                                                                                                                            To my understanding, ...is/are supposed to pay more attention to ...

                                                                                                                                            Meanwhile/at the same time, it is essential/advisible for sb. to work out comprehensive strategies to...

                                                                                                                                            • 同时，某人有必要/应当制定综合策略来...

                                                                                                                                            表达期待

                                                                                                                                            I am looking forward to...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I am looking forward to your favorable reply.

                                                                                                                                            I hope to/that...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I sincerely hope that you can participate in the contest.

                                                                                                                                            号召

                                                                                                                                            Everyone should bear in mind that...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Thus, everyone should bear in mind that "Never give up. You will eventually achieve your dreams".

                                                                                                                                            Society as a whole should foster a positive atmosphere to...

                                                                                                                                            概述图表

                                                                                                                                            Given/This is a column chart that clearly illustrates the dramatic/significant changes in..., from...(start time)to...(end time)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：This is a column chart that clearly illustrates the dramatic changes in shares of domestic(代指中国), American, and Japanese car brands in the Chinese market from 2008 to 2009

                                                                                                                                            • the share of：某某的份额，如果是多者则share要变成shares

                                                                                                                                            • 除了column chart，还可以是：table（表格）、pie chart（饼图）、line graph（曲线图）

                                                                                                                                            Overall, as can be seen from this column chart, ...changed significantly from ...(time1) to ...(time2).

                                                                                                                                            动态图表的变化

                                                                                                                                            上升：...(time), the share of... ascended remarkably, reaching approximately...(data)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：During the period, the share of domestic brands ascended remarkably, reaching approximately 33%.

                                                                                                                                            下降：...(time), the share of... descended remarkably, from its peak at...(data1) to ...(data2)

                                                                                                                                            ...displayed a sharp increase/decline, from ...(data1) to ...(data2)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：By the contrast, sales of Japanese brands displayed a sharp decline, form 35% to 26%.

                                                                                                                                            The number/percentage of ... maintained/remained stable at ...(data)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Meanwhile, the percentage of American brands maintained stable at 12%.

                                                                                                                                            静态图表的占比

                                                                                                                                            Overall, ... is/have the largest proportion of..., at...(data)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Overall, seniors have the largest proportion of part-time job takers, at 88.24%.

                                                                                                                                            The next are...and..., with ...(data) and...(data) respectively.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：The next are juniors and sophomores, with 71.93% and 71.13% respectively.

                                                                                                                                            Finally come(s)..., at...(data)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Finally come freshmen, at 66.77%.

                                                                                                                                            By contrast, the statistic of ..., at merely ...(data), is the lowest among all groups.

                                                                                                                                            引出原因

                                                                                                                                            But what resulted/incurred in the differences above? To my understanding, ...(number) reasons can account for this phenomenon.

                                                                                                                                            描述普遍现象

                                                                                                                                            Similarly/However, in reality, a host of people...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：However, in reality, a host of young people tend to give up when encountering challenges during their pursuit of success.

                                                                                                                                            Similarly/However, in real life, it is not uncommon for people to ...(actions)

                                                                                                                                            描述危害

                                                                                                                                            ...may constitute an obstacle that hinders...(grow/develop...)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：As a matter of fact, the thought of "giving up" may constitute an obstacle that hinders many people's growth and progress in work, life, and study.

                                                                                                                                            只关心...却忽视了...

                                                                                                                                            ...focus/concentrate attention only on..., ignoring/overlooking/neglecting...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：This trend is particularly worth concerning for the reason that they concentrate attention only on the virtual world, overlooking the real life.

                                                                                                                                            举例

                                                                                                                                            A case in point is.../A typical example is...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：A typical example is myself.

                                                                                                                                            ..., including/such as...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：We should utilize modern technological products rationally, including the Internet, cell phones, TV, and computers.

                                                                                                                                            对比反差/引出观点

                                                                                                                                            Although..., ...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Although they gather to enhance friendship, scarcely do these guys chat with each other.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Although I have no children, I cannot agree more with it.

                                                                                                                                            观点+比喻

                                                                                                                                            To my understanding, ...can be likened to/seen as...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：To my understanding, persistence can be seen as a road leading to success.

                                                                                                                                            个人观点

                                                                                                                                            As a ..., I firmly deem/am convinced/cling/believe to the idea that...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：As a senior who is preparing for the national entrace exams for postgraduate studies, I am firmly convinced that perseverance always brings me power...

                                                                                                                                            As a..., I prefer to do...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：As a youngster, I prefer to work in the city and live in the countryside, for...

                                                                                                                                            From my perspective of view...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：From my perspective of view, with these measures taken, students' physical condition will be enhanced.

                                                                                                                                            图表/图画联系主旨

                                                                                                                                            The picture/cartoon, apparently, can be associated/connected with...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：This drawing, apparently, can be associated with two distinct attitude in front of adversities.

                                                                                                                                            Such statistics can be naturally associated/connected with...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Such statistics can be associated with the fierce competition in China's car market.

                                                                                                                                            Such statistics are to reveal...

                                                                                                                                            典型特征（+结果）

                                                                                                                                            ...be marked/characterized by

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：College life is characterized by a rich variety of activities.

                                                                                                                                            A/Some typical characteristic(s)/trait(s) of ... is/are/was/were ...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Some typical characteristics of successful people are diligence, optimism, confident, a strong will and perseverance.

                                                                                                                                            ...be characterized by..., which makes/enables...to...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：in recent years, developing countries were characterized by their economic boom, which makes residents here to possess additional money to purchase mobile phone.

                                                                                                                                            对比（+结果）

                                                                                                                                            Compared with..., ...

                                                                                                                                            • 第二个句子用比较级或含比较意义的表达，如not adequate，superfluous等

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Compared with students' demand in study, the number and variety of books are not adequate.

                                                                                                                                            In comparison to/with...

                                                                                                                                            in contrast to...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：The Sino-Japanese relations got worse in contrast to the past.

                                                                                                                                            积极意义（排比句）

                                                                                                                                            ...will bring sb. ..., arouse sb.'s enthusiasm/passion for..., and help sb. to ...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Practice and diligence will bring you experience and inspiration, arouse your enthusiasm for study, and help you to make progress every day.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：I am firmly convinced that perseverance always brings me power, arouses my enthusiasm for the future, and helps me to conquer so many hardships.

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Studying abroad brings young people more opportunities, arouses their enthusiasm for foreign cultures, and helps them broaden their horizons.

                                                                                                                                            概述图画/材料

                                                                                                                                            This is a simple but enlightening picture, which vividly illustrates/depicts a common phenomenon among...

                                                                                                                                            • 这是一张简单但富有启发性的图画，生动展示了存在于...之中的一个普遍现象

                                                                                                                                            This is a simple but enlightening excerpt of an article, in which the auther argues that...

                                                                                                                                            • 这是一篇简单但富有启发性的文章节选，作者在文章中指出...

                                                                                                                                            This insightful and thought-provoking picture vividly depicts a common phenomenon among...

                                                                                                                                            • 这张富有洞见且引人深思的图画，生动描绘了存在于...之中的一个普遍现象

                                                                                                                                            描述图画细节（时间地点人物动作特征）

                                                                                                                                            ...(time and place), there be ...(people/item), ...(actions), ...with ...(characteristics)

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：On the path to the top of a high mountain, there is a youngster sitting for rest and sighing, "I am too tired to climb".

                                                                                                                                            文字说明

                                                                                                                                            Several Chinese characters can be noticed below the picture, which say that...

                                                                                                                                            • 例句：Two Chinese characters（汉字） can also be noticed below the picture, which say "on the journey"
